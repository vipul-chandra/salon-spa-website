About Us

Our Story

At Vipasha Salon, our story is one of passion, creativity, and a commitment to making you look and feel your absolute best. Founded with a vision to redefine beauty and self-care, our journey began 2010. From our humble beginnings, we've grown into a thriving salon, serving the Rohtak community with pride. Our story is all about you - our valued clients. We've dedicated ourselves to creating a welcoming and luxurious space where you can escape the daily grind and indulge in the pampering you deserve. With each visit, you become a part of our story, and we're grateful for the trust you place in us to enhance your natural beauty and boost your confidence.
